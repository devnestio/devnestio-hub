Standard JWT Claims (RFC 7519)

    
issIssuer — who issued the token

    
subSubject — who the token refers to

    
audAudience — who the token is intended for

    
expExpiration time (Unix timestamp)

    
nbfNot before (Unix timestamp)

    
iatIssued at (Unix timestamp)

    
jtiJWT ID — unique identifier

    
algAlgorithm (header) — e.g. HS256, RS256

    
typType (header) — usually "JWT"
